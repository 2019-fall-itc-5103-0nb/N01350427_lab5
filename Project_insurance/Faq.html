What types of insurance does RA Insurance offer?

RA Insurance is Canada’s largest personal and business insurance company. We offer auto, home insurance through a network of brokers across Canada.

How can we help you today?

At RA, our main goals are to give you the information you need to put you at the center of every decision, and to give you advice to help protect what’s important to you. With hundreds of local experts, you can rely on our trusted advice and all the support you deserve.

How long do I have access?

You have lifetime access to the plan. In other words, it never expires. Months, even years from now you can re-use the plan without paying again.

How does the refund policy works?

If you're unhappy for any reason, email us within 30 days of purchasing on the web, and we will give you a full refund. No strings attached.

What can I do to make sure that I have enough insurance?

Your insurance broker will assess your needs based on the details you provide about your home or vehicle. In case of home insurance, a complete inventory of your belongings will help you get the right coverage and make it easier to file a claim.
